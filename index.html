About Soundballs

Welcome to Soundballs — an experimental audio-visual playground where music and motion collide!

This is an interactive webpage that combines visuals with music theory to generate music through bouncing balls. Each ball triggers different notes based on collisions, creating unique melodic patterns every time. The soundscapes created are suitable for focus/sleep/relaxation.

🚀 Coming Soon:

  • 🎵 Ambient background soundscapes
  • 🎵 More visual effects and color themes
  • 🎵 Apply cool presets and save your own combinations

Sound Balls is constantly evolving. Check back for updates and new features!
